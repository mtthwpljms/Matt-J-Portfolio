About Me

Hi! My name is Matthew James. I am a Web Developer based in Atlanta.

In 2018, I graduated from Columbus State University with a Bachelors in Business Marketing. From there, I moved from my hometown of Columbus to Atlanta where I bought a house, got married, and spent about 2 years working a marketing job. It quickly became apparent however that marketing was not a field in which I felt fulfilled. Once I finally had the confidence to make a career change, I came across the Coding Bootcamp offered through the Georgia Institute of Technology. Throughout the coding bootcamp, I have learned many useful and fulfilling web development skills that I feel passionate about such as HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

In my free time, I enjoy making music, playing video games, and of course, making web applications to entertain others or to help make life a bit easier.
